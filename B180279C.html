About Us

Our Mission

FEIT has set the following objectives in its establishment:

  • To provide high-quality and all round education in engineering and information technology to produce competent engineers and IT professionals capable of dealing effectively with sophisticated engineering and IT problems.
  • Increase activities to promote innovation, commercialization and entrepreneurship in the field of software and electronics engineering development.
  • To help the country in achieving its goal as a developed country in 2020 in terms of the number of professionals in the fields of engineering and ICT.
